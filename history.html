History of Tetris

The history of teris is interesting. The creator was Alexey Pajitnov. He originally created the game
on a massive mainframe computer. He based the game off of some of the puzzels he liked as a kid,
but made modifications when the game got really boring. The game was technicaly owned by the soviet union
but Pajitnov eventually got ownership of the game.

  • Tetris for the gameboy is one of the best selling videogames of all time
